About Us

Baba Decor Hub is the leading Brand of Hospitality and Catering/Banquet Products.

Welcome to Baba Decor Hub, the leading manufacturer in India for Hotels, Banquets, and Catering Products. We specialize in a wide array of products including Buffet Counters, Chafing Dishes, Dining Furniture, Ready-to-Use Banquet Interiors, Banquet Gates, Trolleys, Tray Jacks, Dustbins, Bowls, Risers, Platters, and more, catering specifically to the Ehoreca Industry.
